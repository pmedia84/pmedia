Why Does My Business Need A Facebook Page?

That is a very good question! The answer is a simple one, with Facebook being the go to place for the vast majority of people it makes sense to have some kind of exposure to this vast opportunity. Here are a few reasons that might help you decide.

Engaging With Clients

Facebook is viewed so many times a day by your potential clients that it would be a shame to miss out on this potential. Not only can you find prospective clients, you can engage with your existing ones too

Grow Your Brand Awareness

Once, you have your page established and set up you can promote your business on the various groups and local pages that there are on Facebook. Even a simple post showing what you do on a daily basis can make all the difference to your business.

Communication Platform

Facebook has many free business tools at your fingertips, such as the Business Suite, Messaging and WhatsApp. You can use these tools for free and they help your future and present clients contact you with confidence straight from Facebook.

Advertising

Facebook also offers advertising plans for all it's business users. So not only can you build your brand awareness you can also run advertising campaigns that will enhance your reach even further.

How Do I Get Started?

Creating a Facebook Page for your business is incredibly easy and you can do it straight from your personal profile.

Creating A Facebook Page For Your Business

Click On Create Page

From here you can then fill out all the information about your business

Make sure to fill out all the information in as much detail you can. The more information that you place on these pages will help future clients make that all important decision to contact you.

Next Steps

Once you have filled out the basic information about your business and assigned any admins that you may want, you will then need to upload a cover image and a logo.

Cover Images

A great designed cover image will greatly enhance your page and give a great first impression for your business. You just need to make sure it follows a few simple guidelines as specified by Facebook 

You can design your own cover image using most graphic design software such as Figma or Canva, they both offer some great templates and inspiration to get you started 

Profile Image

Your profile image is very important, you don't want to leave this blank. It will help you create that brand identity and help future clients remember you. Keep it clean and simple and follow the guidelines.

Get Posting.....

Congratulations, you have finished making a great first step in your business' social media journey. You can post to your hearts content and help your business grow.
